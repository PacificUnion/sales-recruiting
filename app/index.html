Our History

We’ve grown from year to year, success to success. And we’ve done it with great people. Come work with the best of the best.

Pacific Union Financial Founded by Evan Stone

Evan Stone saw a hard-working underserved population of people, who were locked out of traditional homeownership, and he decided to do something about it. So he founded a company that reaches those who couldn’t be reached before.

Wholesale Channel Added

Wholesale is a crucial part of our business. We’re one of the Top 25 Broker Channels in the US, after all, and we know that success is rooted in making sure we work as hard for our partners as we do for our people and our borrowers.

Correspondent Channel Added

When opportunity knocked, we opened the door. Pacific Union is now one of the Top 15 Correspondent lenders in the market by volume – and we intend to keep climbing.

Rick Skogg Appointed CEO

Rick Skogg brought his 27 years of mortgage experience to Pacific Union and immediately started making things happen. Under his guidance, all of our channels have excelled. And we plan to keep this trend going for a long, long time.

In-House Loan Servicing Added

At Pacific Union, we know that if you want something done right, you sometimes need to do it yourself. So we do. In-house loan servicing means we can help our borrowers from the beginning to the end of the mortgage process.

Distributed Retail Channel Added,
Servicing Channel Added &
$1 Billion Funded Volume

Originating loans should be detailed, but not difficult. The addition of our Distributed Retail channel makes it easy to build community relationships and reach borrowers across the country. To defy the status quo and provide a better option for home buyers to turn to directly, Pacific Union decided to add Distributed Retail branches. No wonder we broke the billion-dollar funded volume barrier.

Monthly Funded Volume Topped $1.4 Billion
&
Retail Channel Volume Increased by 545% over 2014

Funding loans means we’ve succeeded in putting great people in great homes – with the assistance of great Loan Officers. That’s the reason for our funded volume growth and our Retail Channel expansion. It’s all about our people.

Servicing Portfolio exceeds $21 Billion

As a full-service mortgage company, we’re proud to provide exemplary servicing for loans across our Retail, Wholesale, and Correspondent channels. That’s why we continue to break our own records year after year after year.

FlexKey

  • Restart Program: Minimum FICO 560
  • Expanded Program: Minimum FICO 600
  • Fully Amortized Fixed Rate and 5/1 LIBOR ARM; Fixed Rate Interest Only
  • Cash-out available on Primary Residence, Second Home and Investment
  • Reduced seasoning on Bankruptcy, Foreclosure, Short Sale and Deed-In-Lieu
  • Non-warrantable condos
  • Non-occupying co-borrowers
  • Manual Underwriting required

FHA

  • Fixed Rate and ARM available
  • Manufactured Homes are eligible
  • Non-Credit Qualifying streamline refinances allowed
  • Gift funds allowed for down payment and closing costs
  • Minimum credit score: 560 (with the exception of Non-Credit Qualifying transactions)
  • Escrow Holdback allowed
  • HUD REO
  • Manual Underwrites
  • Blended ratios allowed
  • Limited 203(k) Rehabilitation loans and 203(h) Disaster Victim loans allowed

VA

  • Minimum credit score: 560
    (with the exception of VA IRRRL transactions)

  • Manual Underwrites
  • Fixed Rate and ARM available
  • Escrow Holdback allowed

Conventional

  • Fannie Mae HomeReady®
  • Freddie Mac Home Possible®
  • Minimum credit score: 620
  • Escrow Holdback allowed
  • Blended ratios allowed

USDA

  • Minimum credit score: 581
  • 100% maximum LTV
  • Manual Underwrites
  • No maximum loan amount
  • USDA Streamlined-Assist: no credit report, DTI ratio, appraisal for existing guaranteed loans, or property inspection required
  • Streamlined and Rate/Term refinances allowed
  • Manufactured Homes are eligible

Jumbo
